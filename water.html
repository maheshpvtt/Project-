Water Pollution Awareness

What is Water Pollution?

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances contaminate water bodies, making them unsafe for humans and aquatic life.

Causes of Water Pollution

  • Industrial Waste
  • Oil Spills
  • Plastic Pollution
  • Sewage and Wastewater
